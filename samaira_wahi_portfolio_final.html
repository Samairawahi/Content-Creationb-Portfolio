How I Create Content That Hits

Great content starts with a great hook. Here are the frameworks I use to create videos that stop the scroll — for my own page and for brand campaigns.

Hook Type · 01
"Nobody talks about this but every college student needs to know it."
The secret reveal hook. Creates instant curiosity and positions the content as exclusive knowledge. Works for finance, lifestyle, and product education.
Hook Type · 02
"I tracked every rupee I spent in college for 30 days. Here's what actually shocked me."
The experiment/result hook. Real data + personal story = instant trust. Ideal for finance brands, apps, and lifestyle products.
Hook Type · 03
"The thing about [brand/product] that no one tells you in the ad."
The honest review hook. Feels anti-ad, which makes it the most powerful ad. High saves, high shares, high trust — perfect for UGC.
Idea · 01
Day-in-my-life with a brand integrated naturally
Not a promo. A real day — with your product showing up the way it actually would. Feels authentic. Converts better.
Idea · 02
"What I wish I knew" finance series
Educational content that's emotionally resonant. Perfect for fintech, banking, investment apps targeting students.
Idea · 03
Before & after / transformation storytelling
Show a problem, introduce the product as the shift, show the result. Simple, effective, high-converting UGC format.
Idea · 04
POV: You're a broke college student who found this
POV format creates immediate identification. Your audience sees themselves in the story before they even hear the pitch.
